People Are Frequently Asking Some Questions

We Provide Companies and Individuals With Transparent, Efficient and Sustainable Land Management Through Real-Time Access and Advanced GIS Visualization

What is Digital Land and Assets Management System (DLAMS)?

Our DLAMS is a digital platform that provides users with secure, real-time access to land records, property details, and geospatial visualizations.

How do I access my property information?

Simply log in to your account, and you'll be able to search for and view your property details, including title documents, land records maps and other attributes.

Can I view my property on a map?

Yes, our platform offers interactive GIS-based maps, allowing you to visualize your land parcels and even buildings in 2D and 3D.

What services does LIMS offer?

We offer land records management, property search, title verification, survey requests, and geospatial visualizations, among other services.

Is my data secure on the platform?

Yes, we use advanced encryption and security protocols to ensure all user data, including property and transaction details, are fully protected.

Can I request a land survey through the platform?

Yes, you can easily request land surveys, assessments, or evaluations directly through the platform and track the status in real-time.

How can I make payments for services?

You can securely pay for services like surveys, and assessments using our integrated online payment system.

What happens if I lose access to my account?

You can recover your account by using the "Forgot Password" feature or contacting our support team for assistance.

Can I transfer land titles through LIMS?

Yes, we offer a streamlined process for transferring land titles, including document submission and legal verification.

Is LIMS accessible on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive, allowing you to access all features from your phone, tablet, or computer.
