Who we are

At Digital Land and Asset Management System-Africa (DLAMS AFRICA), we are redefining property management with a secure, innovative platform that empowers owners to safeguard, manage, and maximize their assets. From secure data storage to seamless inheritance planning and a trusted marketplace, we provide all-in-one solutions for property owners, investors, and developers.

DLAMS isn’t merely a tool; it’s a bold step forward in reimagining land and asset management. Crafted to meet the distinct needs of our clients, DLAMS embodies a new standard of innovation, precision, and streamlined efficiency. Designed to transform the landscape of land management, it stands as a powerful ally for those seeking smarter, more impactful solutions.

Our Journey

Our journey started in 2023, driven by a vision to transform how corporations and government entities manage their vital land assets. Over the years, this unwavering commitment to excellence has fueled our evolution, establishing DLAMS as the premier solution in land and asset management. With each milestone, we continue to lead the industry, setting new standards and delivering unmatched value to our clients.

Our Global Footprint

DLAMS is trusted by more than 100 corporates worldwide, each with unique land management needs and complexities. Our reach includes corporate clients and government institutions, overseeing over 500,000 acres of land and impacting landscapes and communities across regions.

A Global Reach

Our influence spans continents, with clients in India, the Middle East, Africa, and the United States. We excel at customizing solutions to meet the specific demands of our clients, no matter where they are located.
