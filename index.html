About Us

At CodeIcon, we are dedicated to revolutionizing land and property management through technology. Our Digital Land and Assets Management System (DLAMS) is a one-stop solution designed to provide easy access to land records, property details, and visualizations anywhere, anytime. With the goal of bringing transparency, efficiency, and accuracy to property management, our platform combines cutting-edge Geographic Information Systems (GIS) technology with a user-friendly interface to ensure a seamless experience for landowners, real estate developers, government agencies, and surveyors. We empower our users to view and manage their properties with more control and detail than ever before. From detailed land records and title searches to advanced property visualizations, we are committed to simplifying land administration and improving the way people interact with their properties.

Our Vision

We envision a future where landowners, governments, and businesses can seamlessly access and manage land and property data from anywhere, promoting greater transparency and equity in land administration. Our goal is to be at the forefront of digital transformation in land management, setting new standards for accuracy, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations by providing them with a comprehensive, secure, and accessible platform to manage and visualize their land and properties. Through continuous innovation and exceptional customer service, we aim to simplify land administration and contribute to a more efficient and sustainable land management system.
